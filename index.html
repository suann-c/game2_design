Cat and Mouse

by: Suann Chi (suannc)

Tom and Jerry shenanigans with a Tetris-like twist.

Tom wants to destroy Jerry by knocking random objects off a shelf. Jerry has to survive by not getting hit.

Gameplay

The game starts off with Tom having a random amount of objects to throw down at Jerry. Tom can choose where to drop the objects. The object will disappear once it hits the floor or it hits Jerry. Jerry attempts to survive by going left or right to dodge the object coming down at him. Tom wins when he hits Jerry. Jerry wins if he manages to dodge all of Tom's objects.
